About Arkraft Technica

Arkraft Technica is a legally registered technology company operating under the laws of the Republic of Kenya. We exist to design and build a resilient digital ecosystem where decentralization is the core principle, not an afterthought.

Our products are engineered to adapt to the dynamic realities of emerging and developing regions — including inconsistent connectivity, regulatory complexity, and evolving digital risks.

We prioritize data privacy, protection, and sovereignty, ensuring that users and organizations retain ownership and control of their data, infrastructure, and communications.

Arkraft Technica builds systems that remain functional, secure, and independent — regardless of platform control, network conditions, or external interference.

Overview of Our Products

Arkraft Technica provides software products with different technical architectures and intended use cases. Data handling, responsibilities, and limitations vary by product.

TARK

TARK is an enterprise communication platform offered on a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) basis and is intended exclusively for organizations and their authorized users.

Arkraft Technica does not monitor message content or internal user activity within TARK. Communication data and content are stored and controlled on infrastructure designated by the client organization.

Arkraft Technica may process limited administrative information such as client identity, subscription details, billing records, and aggregate user counts solely for service delivery and support.

Qwerty Chat

Qwerty Chat is a free-to-use, peer-to-peer messaging application that enables nearby users to communicate using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology.

Qwerty Chat does not use centralized servers, user accounts, or cloud storage. Messages and related data are stored locally on users’ devices. Arkraft Technica does not collect, access, store, or monitor communications conducted through Qwerty Chat.

Due to its decentralized design, Arkraft Technica has no technical ability to moderate, recover, or control user communications within Qwerty Chat.
