About Arkraft Technica

Arkraft Technica is a legally registered technology company operating under the laws of the Republic of Kenya. We exist to design and build a resilient digital ecosystem where decentralization is the core principle, not an afterthought.

Our products are engineered to adapt to the dynamic realities of emerging and developing regions — including inconsistent connectivity, regulatory complexity, and evolving digital risks.

We prioritize data privacy, protection, and sovereignty, ensuring that users and organizations retain ownership and control of their data, infrastructure, and communications.

Arkraft Technica builds systems that remain functional, secure, and independent — regardless of platform control, network conditions, or external interference.

Systems & Solutions

An ecosystem designed around privacy, user control, and independence

Arkraft Technica exists to solve a growing global problem—the loss of privacy, control, and independence in digital communication. In a world dominated by centralized platforms, users and organizations are increasingly disconnected from ownership of their data, infrastructure, and their right to communicate freely.

Our objective is to enable secure, resilient communication that remains operational regardless of network availability, platform restrictions, or external control. By combining modern system design with decentralized technologies, we empower individuals and enterprises to communicate on their own terms.

Independent of proprietary platform dependencies

Tark

Tark enables institutions to deploy, operate, and fully govern their own digitally sovereign communications infrastructure. Designed for mission-critical environments, the platform delivers secure, interoperable, and scalable messaging and collaboration capabilities while preserving full data ownership and administrative control. Tark supports compliance with internal governance policies and regulatory requirements, integrates seamlessly with existing IT ecosystems, and provides long-term operational resilience without dependence on third-party platforms or external service providers.

Qwerty Chat

Next-gen messaging app that lets you talk freely, even without the internet. It uses Bluetooth LE to connect nearby users, and when you’re online, it can route messages through Tor for extra privacy. No central servers, no gatekeepers, just decentralized, resilient communication that works anywhere and puts control back in your hands.
