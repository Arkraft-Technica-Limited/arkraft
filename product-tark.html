About Arkraft Technica

Arkraft Technica is a legally registered technology company operating under the laws of the Republic of Kenya. We exist to design and build a resilient digital ecosystem where decentralization is the core principle, not an afterthought.

Our products are engineered to adapt to the dynamic realities of emerging and developing regions — including inconsistent connectivity, regulatory complexity, and evolving digital risks.

Arkraft Technica builds systems that remain functional, secure, and independent — regardless of platform control, network conditions, or external interference.

Security & Access

Tark is built with security and access control at its core. Leveraging end-to-end encryption (E2EE) and open, audited protocols, Tark ensures that communications remain private by default and accessible only to authorized participants. Institutions retain full control over user access, identity management, and data ownership, enabling secure collaboration without reliance on external service providers. Designed to operate across both private and federated environments, Tark supports secure authentication, role-based access controls, and resilient connectivity, ensuring trusted communication across devices, teams, and organizational boundaries.

Opensource Ecosystem

Tark is built on open-source technology, delivering transparency, auditability, and independence from proprietary systems. By relying on openly reviewed and community-driven code, Tark allows users and institutions to verify how the platform operates, adapt it to their specific needs, and deploy it with full confidence. Designed for everyday use, the app enables secure communication and collaboration across mobile Android (and soon desktop) environments, combining strong, privacy-first security with a simple, intuitive user experience that supports efficient and reliable collaboration at scale.

Deploy Tark Your Way

Tark is built for organizations that demand control. You can deploy and operate Tark entirely within your own infrastructure, maintaining full ownership of your data, identity systems, and communication flows without reliance on external platforms.

For teams that prefer speed and simplicity, Arkraft Technica also offers fully managed Tark server plans. We handle infrastructure, security hardening, monitoring, and uptime while you retain sovereignty over users, data, and policies.

Whether self-hosted or managed, Tark delivers enterprise-grade, decentralized communication with strong encryption, federation control, and resilience against outages, restrictions, and centralized failure points.

Our server plans are designed to scale with your organization, from private internal deployments to multi-node, high-availability systems. Support, onboarding, and remote technical assistance are available to ensure smooth deployment and long-term reliability.
